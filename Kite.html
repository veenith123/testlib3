READING

A Kite on the ground

Is just paper and string

But up in the air

It will dance and sing.

A kite in the air

Will dance and caper

But back on the ground

Is just string and paper

Meaning Words

String:

A set of fibre's tied or threaded

together to form a thin rope.

Caper:

Dance about in a lively or playful way.
